New Years Resolution: A Commit a Day - 2016

One of my New Years Resolutions (apart from making more mod videos and working out/eating healthier) is to at least commit to one of my projects at least once a day. I am planning to spend at least 30 minutes per day for each of these commits. A "commit" can be something pretty simple - for example some Bootstrap templates I'm working on, and perhaps in the future I might start just commiting some markdown files on programming practices or other relating things - for example perhaps a markdown guide to using photoshop, or making resources smaller (images) for websites that serve a lot of images that could be compressed better.

Mainly I am doing this because it would be cool to see the progress in a project over this year and to sharpen my programming skills. Most programmers know that continous learning and improvement is just something that comes with our career path.
